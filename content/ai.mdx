# AI

**At Urban Tech Creative, we leverage AI as a catalyst for co-creation, always enhancing rather than replacing human narratives.** 

In projects like [Window to the Soul](/work/window-to-the-soul), we used AI to anonymize genuine refugee testimonies, preserving authentic voices while ensuring dignity. Similarly, in [Dock Stories](/work/dock-stories), AI empowered digital characters anchored in local heritage. Across our placemaking and immersive storytelling, AI supports photogrammetry pipelines, adaptive AR content, and personalized experiences — always ensuring **authenticity and collaboration** remain at the core.
