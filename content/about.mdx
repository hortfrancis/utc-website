# About

<Section id='vision'>
  ## Our Vision

We are a collaborative creative tech design studio focusing on innovative storytelling using the latest technology.

Born from the unique fusion of architectural and game development studio, our focus is to enable you to deliver engaging, exciting and accessible narratives to your audiences.

We work across the skillsets of game development, augmented reality, spatial computing, urban design and architecture to deliver a unique approach to experience design.

</Section>

<Section id='team'>
  ## Team

Our founder James Lee Burgess practiced as a building design Architect and Urban Designer for over 20 years and is now a qualified developer.

His passion is working with clients and their teams to deliver the best possible creative outcomes. James founded Urban Tech Creative after seeing the huge potential technology had to enhance our sense of place and also tell stories in a new way with 3d digital design interacting with the real world around us.

James is a Digital Poverty Alliance Ambassador, a full member of the Association of Project Managers and a Fellow of the Royal Society of the Arts.

</Section>
