# BT Urban.AR

**Urban.AR**, the first smart city engagement tool that can utilise a combination of live and contained data feeds depending on the client requirements.

For British Telecom (BT) we created the a London Phygital Edition, with multiple areas of Smart City focus overlaid on a high quality physical scale model.

Urban.AR can cover any town or city by providing a bespoke service within our augmented reality templates.
