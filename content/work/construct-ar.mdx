# Construct.AR

With our origins in Architectural Practice, helping advance the Built Environment sectors’ ability to deliver better and safer design is one of our passions.

We do this by enabling teams with Augmented and Mixed Reality capabilities, delivering this work under our **Construct.AR** approach.

Construct.AR uses Trimble Geosystems technology and software to deliver mixed and augmented reality solutions for our clients. We offer a client advisory services, modelling solutions and education outreach.

Our work includes working alongside the construction teams at a new education facility for City College Norwich called Digi-Tech Factory, refurbishment of the Grade 2 listed Norwich Castle Museum and Rochester Bridges Trust tracking existing services.

Construct.AR can help mitigate pre-construction risks, assist site setting out and problem solving, offer remote assist from office to site, and track existing estate assets.

Construct.AR uses ipads, mobiles or the ground breaking Trimble Hololens site ready mixed reality headsets.
